How to Print on Receipt Paper

If your receipt looks like it's going to print on a 5-mile-long sheet, follow these steps in your browser print settings:

  1. Go to your browser's print menu (press Ctrl+P or Cmd+P)
  2. Under Printer, select your receipt printer (e.g. Star TSP100 Cutter)
  3. Click More Settings >
  4. Change Paper Size to Star Thermal 3inch x Letter
  5. Set Margins to None
  6. Disable Headers and Footers if the option is there
  7. Click Print
TIP: If you dont see the right paper size, IT might have to install proper "Drivers"
